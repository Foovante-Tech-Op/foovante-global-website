How we verify

Every tonne, watched from orbit and the ground.

You shouldn't have to take our word for any credit. Foovante runs a four-layer verification stack: satellite, field, third-party, on-chain. Every funder gets the full audit trail, per tonne.

7+ years of Sentinel-2 imagery for every project polygon. Monitored every 5 days at 10m resolution, automatically.
